1967Shelby Gt500

The 1967 Shelby GT500 was the last car built at the original Shelby American facility in Venice, California. It had supercharged Rush performance 5.0LTi-VCTCoyote DOHC v8 engine with 600 hp.

The drive line had to be upgraded because there was so much horse power and would tear the car apart . It had top notch brakes large six piston forged aluminum calipers with slotted and vented rotors in the front ,

and in the back there where four piston aluminum calipers in the rear. It had stainless steel dual exhaust. it was tuned specifically for the Ford Coyote engine it has a soft idle and is quiet at cruise speed.

It has lower restriction mufflers to provide more power and aggressive tone. It has a twin disc clutch and a dual circuit braking system.
