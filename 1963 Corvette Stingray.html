In 1963 Corvette  came out with a new look it was called Corvette Stingray. There was two different types of models the Corvette newcomer/the Stingray and the Sports Coupe.

The body panels and curved side windows showed the miles ahead aerodynamic design. there were power-operated retractable headlamps that Flip into the unobstructed hood line.

The Corvette engineering features were upgrade significantly. The chassis for example the Corvette’s new frame was stronger and more rigid than other models of the past.

New four wheel independent suspension they helps keeps handling steady. The maintenance was easier there was a battery saving Decontrol generator, extended-life exhaust system and hydraulic self adjusting breaks.
