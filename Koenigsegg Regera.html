Koenigsegg Regera

The Koenigsegg  has a combine engine between a twin turbo v8 and three electric motors. They removed the traditional gear box to make the car lighter and more eficient. 

The combustion engine has 1100hp and 1250 torque and the electric engine has 700hp and 990 torque
